## The Problem

It's 8:47am. The coffee line is 15 deep. Your meeting starts at 9. Sound familiar?

**Brewline** lets you order your usual from the train, pay in-app, and walk straight to pickup. Plus earn rewards with every coffee - because loyalty should be rewarded.

---

## What You Get

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Mobile Ordering** | Order and pay ahead |
| **Customization** | Build your perfect drink |
| **Rewards** | Earn points with every purchase |
| **Find Cafes** | Discover participating locations |
| **ETA** | Know exactly when your order is ready |
| **Stored Payment** | Fast, secure checkout |
